WE ARE PANASONIC PANTHERS

ALWAYS TO THE TOP, ALL WAYS TOGETHER

Panasonic Panthers is an elite men's professional volleyball club based in Osaka, Japan, competing in the top-tier V. League Division 1. The team is one of the most successful in Japan, which owns seven national championships. Featuring strong Japanese national players and talented imports, the Panthers are always considered major title contenders known for their fast, coordinated play.
